About Me

Web developer, drone pilot, hobby photographer.

Hi all, I am Herman Vulkers. I am a starting web developer in Utrecht, The Netherlands. Since a couple of years, I've been dabbling in different coding languages as a hobby. During the COVID pandemic, I decided to take a more serious approach and commit to a career switch, from drone operator to web developer.


During September 2021, I enrolled in a Full Stack Web Development course at Bit-Academy in The Netherlands. Some of the languages that I learned to work with more intensively are HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP.


Some facts about me, in no particular order:


  • For more than a decade, I worked as a professional drone pilot.
  • My favorite computer game when I was 6 years old was Power Pete on our Macintosh Performa.
